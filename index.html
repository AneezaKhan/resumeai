FAQ

Common questions

Is my CV data safe and private? +
Yes. Your CV text is sent to the AI for processing and immediately discarded. We never store, log, or use your CV data for any other purpose. Nothing is ever sold or shared.
How is this different from just using ChatGPT? +
ResumeAI uses a highly specialised prompt built by CV experts — it knows exactly how to structure a CV, which action verbs to use, how to quantify achievements, and how to optimise for ATS systems. It's not a generic AI chat — it's a dedicated career tool.
What does ATS-optimised mean? +
Most large companies use Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) to automatically scan and filter CVs before a human ever sees them. If your CV isn't formatted correctly or doesn't include the right keywords, it gets rejected automatically. Our tool optimises your CV to pass these filters.
Do I need to create an account? +
No. The free tier requires no account, no email, and no credit card. Just open the tool and start. Upgrading to Pro will require an account to manage your subscription.
Can I use this for any industry or job type? +
Yes — ResumeAI works for any industry. Use Professional mode for corporate roles, Technical for engineering and development, Creative for design and media, and Executive for senior leadership positions. Just paste the job description and it adapts.
